# Welcome to Vibe Research

This is your AI-powered deep research platform. Submit research requests through GitHub Issues and receive comprehensive, well-structured research reports.

## How It Works

1. **Create an Issue** using our research request template
2. **AI conducts iterative research** on your topic
3. **Results are published** automatically to this site
4. **Browse and read** your research anytime

## Research Depths

We offer three levels of research depth:

| Level | Iterations | Best For |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Quick Overview | 2 | Fast summaries |
| Standard Research | 4 | Balanced coverage |
| Deep Dive | 6 | Comprehensive analysis |

## Getting Started

Ready to start your first research? [Create a research request](https://github.com/your-org/VibeResearch/issues/new/choose) now!
